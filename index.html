My Silly Poem

by Kai Thomas


Silly Poem

*

Hi, my name is Kai

*

I Am a Guy

*

Sometimes I Cry

*

At Sad Movies

*

Ain't Gonna Lie

*

But Whenever I Get Teary Eyed

*

I Go Get Me Some Pie

*

But Do I Have Time?

*

For It's 3.14159.. And On And On Until I Die!

*


Thanks for reading my poem!
